Welcome to Victory Living Word Church

Victory Living Word Church is a dynamic, Word-based ministry located in Kawanda, Uganda. Under the leadership of Pastor Kitondekigya Richard and his family, we are committed to transforming lives through the unfailing Word of our Lord Jesus Christ.

"And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb, and by the word of their testimony..." — Revelation 12:11

Our mission is clear: to raise ministers, equip believers, and spread the Gospel to Kawanda and beyond. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit. Whether you are seeking spiritual growth, healing, or a community of faith, you have a place here.
